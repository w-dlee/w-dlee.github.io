About me

Hi! I'm Dawon Lee. Now, I am a research engineer at the Defense & Safety Convergence Research Division, Digital Convergence Research Laboratory, ETRI, Korea. My research interest is Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and especially to make a computational model using data extracted from an interaction between a user and a system. My goal is to give an improved experience to a user. If you wanna talk with me, feel free to contact me.
