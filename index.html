About me

I am a Ph.D. student at KAIST's Visual Media Lab, supervised of Prof. Junyong Noh. I previously worked under the supervision of Prof. Byungjoo Lee from 2019 to 2020, before transitiong to work with Prof. Junyong Noh. I received my Master's degree at KAIST, also under the supervision of Prof. Junyong Noh. My research interests span across the fields of human-computer interaction (HCI), computer graphics (CG), and multimedia. I am particularly focused on developing techniques to address challenges faced by users during the viewing or production of image and video content. My ultimate goal is to leverage these techniques to enhance the quality of people's lives. If you'd like to get in touch, feel free to contact me!
